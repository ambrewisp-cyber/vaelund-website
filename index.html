What to expect

Dungeons

Grouped dungeons with real difficulty. Bring friends or don't, your call.

Raids

Grouped raids with coordinated fights and boss encounters that require tactics.

PvP

Fight other players in open world PvP. There are no safe zones.

Auction House

Buy and sell with other players. The economy is player-driven.

Leveling

Progress your character, gain levels, unlock abilities and get stronger.

Mounts

Ride mounts to move faster, and look better doing it.

Crafting

Blacksmithing, enchanting, and more. Craft your own gear and sell what you make.

Play with friends

Massively multiplayer, join forces and run content together.

Public and private servers

Play on official servers or run your own. Public for randoms, private for friends.

Moddable

You can write or use mods to change the game and make it yours.
