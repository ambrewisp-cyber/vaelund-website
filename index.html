What you're walking into

⚔

Weighty combat

Every swing means something. Mobs that react, stagger, and flee. Damage that accumulates. Death that costs time.

🏹

One world, no zones

Walk from Millhaven to the Hollow's edge without a loading screen. The world is seamless — and so is the danger.

📜

Quests that connect

A main arc with real stakes. Side stories that tie back. Factions with conflicting agendas. Finish one thing, another thing changes.

🗺️

No hand-holding

150x150 world. Dungeons without quest markers. Secrets for people who look.

⚡

Abilities, not stats

Spell combos. Positional effects. Cooldowns you manage. Build defines how you fight, not just how hard you hit.

🌑

The Hollow grows

Not a raid event. A slow apocalypse. The Seals are weakening. No one has fixed that yet.
